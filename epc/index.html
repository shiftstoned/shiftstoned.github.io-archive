About the probabilities

The number of draws represent cards drawn beyond the opening hand.

The probabilities in the table represent only the odds of drawing power and influence sources, without a mulligan. The requirements for playing those sources isn't considered for the purposes of computing odds. For example, Vara's Favor is considered to be a source of both one power and one shadow influence for the purposes of the probability table, but the prerequisite of two power and one shadow influence, in order to play Vara's Favor, isn't a factor in the calculation.

The original source for Eternal Power Calculator is available on Github.
