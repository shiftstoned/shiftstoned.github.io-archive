Toot the Horn: March 2020 updates Navigating your brew to Eternal Warcry from the fan-built Eternal Power Calculator has never been easier.
March 4, 2020 - Keen-eyed explorers have already spotted some updated features in the fan-built Eternal Power Calculator. With the touch of a button, Eternal brewers can now automatically load and view their lists into the Eternal Warcry deck builder directly from Shiftstoned's Power Base visualization tool.



The updated control panel features a new war horn icon      that will launch an Eternal Warcry tab and load your list directly into the deck builder. Any edits you made to your Power Base, Main Deck or Market in the Eternal Power Calculator will be preserved.




Card Art
In addition, the Power Base visualization tool now includes visuals of a different kind. Brewers have been asking for card art to display when hovering over card names in decklists, and this feature is now fully implemented.



Any edits you make will automatically be included when you choose to share or export the decklist. (Note that Power sources in your Market intentionally do not count towards your overall Power and Influence totals).

We welcome any feedback on these new features, along with any suggestions for future features or updates.

More fan-built features and enhancements for Shiftstoned are in the planning stages.
